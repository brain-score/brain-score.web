Get Involved!

Brain-Score started initially out of a research lab in Cambridge, MA in 2017, and since has grown to incorporate hundreds of users across dozens of institutions.

We also have an active support and development team that is dedicated to making Brain-Score a seamless and fun experience. We constantly try to get community feedback on what is important, quality of life changes, and where the platform should head. If you have thoughts about any of these points, please do not hesitate to reach out.

So, whether you just want to stay on top of news and events or you're looking to roll up your sleeves and contribute to our GitHub repositories, there are many ways to participate in the active and growing Brain-Score community.
